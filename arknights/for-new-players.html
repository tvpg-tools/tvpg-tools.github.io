Recruitment Tags

When recruiting operators some tags are better than others. These tiers are determined by how likely they are to result in the recruitment of a higher-rarity operator. The Robot and Starter tags are classified as "Other" because while the operators that they recruit aren't "strong" it may be worthwhile to get them to max Potential.
