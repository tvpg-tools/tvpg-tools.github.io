Recruitment Tags

When recruiting operators some tags are better than others. These tiers are determined by how likely they are to result in the recruitment of a higher-rarity operator. Please keep in mind that certain combinations of tags will increase the chance of getting a higher rarity operator. The Robot and Starter tags are classified as "Other" because while the operators that they recruit aren't "strong" it may be worthwhile to get them to max Potential. "Qualification Tag" ensures that the operator is of a specific rarity when successful - Top Operator will provide a 6☆ operator, Senior Operator will provide a 5☆, and Starter will provide a 2☆ operator.

Note that the time spent searching for certain tags must allow for operators of that type to be found. E.g. When selecting the Robot tag, the maximum search time is 3h 50m as currently all Robots are 1☆.

Initial Base Setup

The Base is the means by which you will both passively, through Factories and Trading Posts, and actively create resources, through the Workshop. Once you have unlocked the Base, it is the author's recommendation that you set the first Factory's product as Precious Metal; this will help ensure a steady supply of LMD via the Trading Post.

As you gain the ability to upgrade the Base, the facilities that you choose to build becomes more important. The most optimal setup is known as the 252 Setup. Click here for more details. The key feature of the 252 Setup is that the only facilities on the right side that are upgraded are the Workshop and the Training Room; this is because currently the facilities on this side of the base cannot be downgraded. By doing this you are able to operate your Factories and Trading Posts at their maximum output. Once you've built the second level of the base, it is recommended to build 1 Power Plant and 2 Factories - with the Factories set to produce Battle Records. The third level should have 3 Factories - one producing Battle Records, one producing Precious Metal, and the final one producing whatever of the two previous materials that is currently needed most.
